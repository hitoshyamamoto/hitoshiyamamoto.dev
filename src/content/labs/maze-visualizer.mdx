This lab runs entirely in your browser. Pick an algorithm and watch the cells it
explores before reaching the exit; generate a new maze to compare how each
strategy behaves on the same problem.

- **BFS** guarantees the shortest path, but explores wide.
- **DFS** dives deep: fast sometimes, terrible at others.
- **Dijkstra** is BFS with cost (here, uniform cost).
- **A\*** uses the Manhattan heuristic to head straight for the goal.
- **Greedy** follows the heuristic alone: fast, but can miss the optimum.

Watch the **efficiency** metric (path ÷ explored): the higher it is, the less the
algorithm wasted.
