Generates mazes (recursive backtracker, Prim, Kruskal) and runs the solvers side by
side on the same maze (BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, A*, greedy best-first) with comparative
metrics for nodes explored, path length, and efficiency.

It is also the site's inaugural Lab: 100% client-side, no backend, no cold start.
[See the live demo](/en/labs/maze-visualizer).
